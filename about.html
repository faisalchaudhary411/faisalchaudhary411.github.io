Our Mission

VoxCraft Studio was built to democratize AI audio tools. We believe everyone deserves access to high-quality text-to-speech, transcription, and audio editing — without expensive subscriptions or complex software.

The Team

We are a small team of developers and audio engineers passionate about AI. Built on top of Microsoft Edge Neural TTS and open-source audio libraries, VoxCraft delivers professional results directly in your browser.

🚀 Open & Accessible

No signup required. No credit card. No hidden fees. Just powerful AI audio tools that work.

Technology

Powered by Streamlit, Python, edge-tts, pydub, and ffmpeg. Our infrastructure runs on cloud servers with auto-scaling to handle demand. The landing page is built with vanilla HTML and CSS for maximum speed and SEO.

🔒 Privacy First

Your audio files and text inputs are processed in real-time and never stored on our servers. No tracking, no analytics, no data selling.

Built for Creators

Whether you are a YouTuber, podcaster, educator, or developer — VoxCraft gives you the tools to create professional audio content without breaking the bank. From Urdu voiceovers to background music generation, we have got you covered.
