Privacy Policy

How VoxCraft Studio handles your data, audio files, and personal information.

1. Information We Collect

VoxCraft Studio does not require user registration. We process audio and text inputs entirely in your browser session. We do not store your uploaded files, generated audio, or text inputs on our servers permanently.

2. Session Data

We use browser session storage to track monthly character usage for free tier enforcement. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any third-party analytics services.

3. License Verification

If you purchase a Pro license, your license key is verified against LemonSqueezy's secure API. We do not store your payment information — all billing is handled by LemonSqueezy.

4. Advertisements

Free tier users may see advertisements served via our ad network partners. These partners may use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can upgrade to Pro to remove all advertisements permanently.

5. Data Retention

All audio files and text inputs are processed in real-time and are not retained on our servers after your session ends. We do not maintain logs of the content you generate.

6. Contact

For privacy concerns, email: privacy@voxcraft.studio
